About Me:
With a creative career of over 30 years, I began in 1992 as a graphic designer trained in the mechanical production of camera-ready-art for newspapers, magazines, and publications. I witnessed first-hand the technical evolution of the print industry from analog graphic arts to digital desk-top publishing.
After a decade of typesetting, I re-trained in video-production and editing. Working for three network TV stations and several advertising agencies gave me nother decade of experience in script-writing, story-boarding, studio & location-based videography, motion-graphics, 3D modeling and animation.
20 years of print and video led to web-design and interactive, and I found myself authoring web pages and developing flash-based animation and video content. I learned about dynamic data and was hooked!
